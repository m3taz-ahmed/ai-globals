# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in the **AI Global OS** community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community of **engineering excellence**.

---

## Our Standards

### ✅ Behaviors That Strengthen the Community

- Demonstrating respect and precision in technical discussions
- Giving and receiving constructive code review with surgical specificity
- Acknowledging when another approach is better — intellectual honesty is a virtue here
- Focusing criticism on code and ideas, never on individuals
- Sharing hard-won knowledge openly and completely

### ❌ Unacceptable Behaviors

- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks of any kind
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Dismissing contributions without specific, constructive feedback
- Gatekeeping through elitism or condescension
- Repeated off-topic debate or derailing technical discussions

---

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Community leaders are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior and will take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any behavior that they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces — GitHub Issues, Pull Requests, Discussions, and any other venue where an individual is representing the AI Global OS project.

---

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainer via the contact information on the [GitHub profile](https://github.com/m3taz-ahmed).

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

### Enforcement Guidelines

| Level | Behavior | Consequence |
|---|---|---|
| 1 — Correction | Minor unprofessional comment | Private written warning |
| 2 — Warning | Pattern of inappropriate behavior | Public warning, temporary ban |
| 3 — Temporary Ban | Serious violation | 30-day ban from community spaces |
| 4 — Permanent Ban | Sustained harassment or threats | Permanent removal |
